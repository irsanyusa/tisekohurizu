Acid Wash Jeans | Pulse Nigeria

Acid wash jeans were typically paired with neon colours and bold/loud accessories and these days styling them that way come off too much. Acid washed jeans are treated with chemicals that eat away at the pigment and fabric and tend to be softer compared to non-acid washed because the fabric has been 'weakened'

When styling acid wash jeans, avoid pairing them with overly bright colours while its also best to choose them in flattering cuts. You can choose them in black and grey, Light blue, medium or dark blue or coloured.

They are bold, edgy and dramatic, by all means own a pair of acid wash jeans.
