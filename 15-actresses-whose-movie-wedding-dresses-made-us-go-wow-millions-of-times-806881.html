15 Actresses Whose Movie Wedding Dresses Made Us Go Wow Millions of Times

There are so many movies where costume designers did an excellent job. But no costume can be compared to the wedding gowns of our favorite characters. These gowns often become iconic.

At Bright Side, we decided to compare the wedding gowns of the actresses who played brides in movies a few times.

Julia Roberts

Julia Roberts put on a wedding gown for the first time when she was 22 and starred in the drama Steel Magnolias. At 32, she starred in the comedy Runaway Bride, and at 45, she put on a wedding gown again in the movie Mirror Mirror.

Jennifer Lopez

Rachel McAdams

The actress wore very different wedding gowns in the movies The Notebook, The Vow, and The Time Traveler’s Wife.

Natalia Oreiro

We saw Natalia Oreiro wearing a wedding gown in soap operas like Muñeca Brava, Ricos y Famosos and, You Are the One. And it’s hard to say which of the gowns is better.

Amy Adams

Amy Adams was amazing as a bride in the movies Enchanted and The Wedding Day. The gowns don’t look like each other at all, so it’s hard to say which of them suits her better.

Mandy Moore

Alyssa Milano

Alyssa Milano’s character wore a wedding gown more than once in the Charmed TV series. And the gowns were very different.

Leighton Meester

In the Gossip Girl TV series, the actress had to wear 2 different wedding gowns. We are sure that the blue dress inspired many women to get creative with their wedding look.

Kirsten Dunst

Kirsten put on a wedding gown in the movies Mona Lisa Smile and Melancholia.

Kristin Davis

Kristin Davis’s character flaunted flawless wedding dresses in the Sex and the City TV series.

Teri Hatcher

Sarah Jessica Parker

Carrie Bradshaw tried on a few delightful haute couture wedding dresses in the movie Sex and the City before making a choice.

This was the dress Carrie opted for. We believe it was a hard choice for her because other dresses suited her perfectly too.

Anne Hathaway

Katherine Heigl

Katherine Heigl got married on screen twice in the Grey’s Anatomy TV series, and the movie 27 Dresses. It’s interesting that costume designers chose a similar silhouette for her in both projects.

Jennifer Aniston

We saw the actress wearing a wedding gown in the Friends TV series and the movie Marley & Me. Both of them are charming, for sure.

Which wedding gowns from which movies did you like the best? Tell us in the comments below.
