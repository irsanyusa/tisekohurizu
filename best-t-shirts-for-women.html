Best T-shirts for Women: 16 Versatile Options to Add to Your Wardrobe

I have a few perennial quests in my life because I always wonder if there’s something better out there that I missed. The perfect T-shirt is one of them. A simple garment, the T-shirt has been around for a long time—in fact, Hanes started making their white T-shirts in the early 1900s.

Today, pretty much every brand or label has their own version, or two. There are cropped ones, long ones, boxy ones, slim ones, relaxed ones, and more. While there are some great places to order custom T-shirts online, we stuck to solid colored ones (with the exception of one striped) without writing, logos, or images for this list.

To help you wade through all the options, we compiled this guide to the best T-shirts available in a variety of categories. No matter what style you’re looking for, you’re sure to find something that will fit the bill.

What to consider when buying T-shirts for women

There’s a big difference between shopping for a classic white T-shirt that can be dressed up or down, to shopping for a workout shirt. To help with that, consider the following:

Necklines

Crew neck, V-neck, or boat neck: they all offer something. A crew will provide a classic, timeless look while a V-neck adds an elongating detail to your neckline (excellent for making you look a bit taller). Boat necks are great for those with small breasts or pear-shaped bodies.

Materials

Gone are the days where you had to shop for men’s T-shirts in order to find a quality option (especially for white tees). Most of the T-shirts on this list are made from 100% cotton, with a handful being cotton and modal blends. Cotton is a breathable, durable fabric that comes in a variety of weights. I will always love plain old white cotton Hanes T-shirts, they’re the most suitable in my opinion.

Fit and shape

T-shirts come in a variety of fits and shapes including, slim, classic, relaxed, and oversized. Having some in every shape is the way to go. Slim T-shirts are ideal for dressing up or when you want to show off your shape. A classic shape paired with jeans or a skirt creates a stylish, timeless look and a ‘just-right’ oversized fit gives off some vintage vibes.
