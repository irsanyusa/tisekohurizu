Actress says sugar daddy never bought her a multi-million Naira boutique

In an interview with Encomium Weekly, she said she does not even have a shop at all. “That’s a stupid rumour” she said, “there’s nothing like that. I don’t even own a shop let alone a multi-million Naira boutique. I don’t know where they got that.”

She also spoke on the popular belief that a lot of actresses are into prostitution to maintain an expensive lifestyle. “If they say we are actresses into prostitution and that’s why we’re making it in the industry, what will they say of the men in the industry? Are they not making it or they are also prostitutes? So it’s a stupid talk” she said.

Bisola has a 4-year old son, Abdul Malik Ayinde Anifowoshe, with the popular Fuji musician KWAM 1. This year she acquired a Lexus Jeep RX 350 worth four million Naira.
