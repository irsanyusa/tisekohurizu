Pulse List 5 Nollywood movies with best sex scenes and nudity

Though not brazenly graphic, these sex scenes are beautifully captured to push the story forward, and at the same time, excite the audience.

Pulse Movies has put together five tastefully done sex scenes in Nollywood movies.

1. "Fifty"

Though there are a couple of sexy scenes in "Fifty," we’re focusing on the bold scene that went down between Lizzy and her boy toy Sammy.

The scene pushes the boundaries a bit in terms of sex and nudity and are presented in such a way that it is not distasteful.

2. "The Visit"

Ajiri and Lanre's sex is as chaotic and hilarious as their relationship. Yet, we can't but watch in respect at the passionate performances from two talented actors: Nse Ikpe-Etim and Blossom Chukwujekwu.

A four-cast movie, "The Visit" comes with professionally made nudity and hot sex scenes.

3. "Tiwa's Baggage"

One of the things that make "Tiwa's Baggage" memorable is its use of the perfect soundtrack to perfect pivotal scenes such as Tiwa and Lolu's sex scenes.

The several unvoiced emotions and meaningful gestures all come together to give their intimate moments a sense of realness.

4. "Rebecca"

In Shirley Frimpong-Manso's two-cast movie, Clifford and Rebecca engage in a sexual act that is poignant, elevated by the right choice of music.

Stranded in an unknown location, their emotional journey mount up and lead to an intense sex in the rain moment, which is beautifully captured.

5. "Brave"

Without any explicit nudity or even 'penetration,' Layo and Nathan's lovemaking scene in "Brave" is everything and more.

With a soft soundtrack that fits the mood and straightens the couple's emotional connection and needs, the entwined bodies of Layo and Nathan deliver a memorable lovemaking scene.
