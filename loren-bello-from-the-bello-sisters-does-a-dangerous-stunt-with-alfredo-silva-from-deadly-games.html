Loren Bello From The Bello Sisters Does a Dangerous Stunt With Alfredo Silva From Deadly Games

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by Gabe Ginsberg/Getty Images, Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Two of the most jaw-dropping danger acts in recent years joined forces for an unbelievable backstage stunt that left fans breathless. Loren Bello from acrobatic trio The Bello Sisters and knife-thrower Alfredo Silva of Deadly Games pulled off a feat of trust and precision that wowed their followers.

Balancing Act Meets Marksmanship in Nerve-Wracking Stunt

In a behind-the-scenes Instagram video, Loren demonstrated her superhuman balance and strength by lifting one leg overhead while steadying a balloon between her raised foot and mouth. Meanwhile, Silva took aim with his crossbow and, in a moment of suspense, successfully popped the balloon without harming his fellow performer.

The song “Look What You Made Me Do” by Taylor Swift plays as the duo successfully pulled off the stunt. It was undoubtedly risky but also showed immense trust between the two entertainers.

Silva responsibly warned fans not to try the dangerous act themselves, emphasizing the need for proper training and safety. However, for these seasoned professionals, their flawless execution left witnesses astounded.

“We have one more day left, and we decided to do another collaboration just for you,” Silva said. “Deadly Games and Bello Sister let’s go. Don’t try this at home.”

Seeing Loren’s acrobatic finesse meet Silva’s flawless aim was a marvel. It takes immense dedication, courage, and practice to appear so effortless while attempting something so dangerous.

Both Were Previous America’s Got Talent Acts

While Loren and Silva’s collaboration was a shock, their paths crossing shouldn’t be a total surprise. The Bello Sisters first rose to fame on America’s Got Talent in 2020, wowing with contortionist routines showcasing their flexibility.

Since then, they’ve toured Europe and appeared on multiple Got Talent shows abroad. Eldest sister Loren brings both acrobatic and academic prowess, having graduated from school in Italy.

Meanwhile, Silva makes up half of Deadly Games, a danger act that rose to prominence on AGT Season 11. Recognized for daring knife and crossbow acts, Silva’s pinpoint archery skills were on full display in the gasp-inducing backstage stunt with Loren. Both acts clearly have put in endless hours of training to perfect their art.
