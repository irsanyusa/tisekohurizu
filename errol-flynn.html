Errol Flynn (Actor) - On This Day

Profession: Actor

Biography: Errol Flynn was an Australian-born American actor, known for his many roles as a romantic swashbuckler in Hollywood films, as well as for his hedonistic personal life. His handsome appearance, charismatic presence, and adventurous flair made him one of the biggest stars of the Golden Age of Hollywood.

Flynn's breakthrough came with his starring role in Captain Blood (1935), where his performance as a pirate set the stage for a string of similar roles. He went on to star in many successful films, such as The Adventures of Robin Hood (1938), with his name coming to be synonymous with the gallant and heroic characters he portrayed.

Off-screen, Flynn's life was filled with scandal and controversy. He was notorious for his hard-living lifestyle, marked by heavy drinking and high-profile affairs. His autobiography, My Wicked, Wicked Ways, published posthumously, further solidified his reputation as one of Hollywood's most colorful figures.
