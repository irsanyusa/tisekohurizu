Kids FM 101.7 debuts in Lagos

This groundbreaking initiative marks the first of its kind in Nigeria, heralding a new era in children's entertainment and education across the nation. Kids FM 101.7 conducted its test transmission on April 1, with regular transmission slated to commence soon.

According to the CEO Serge Noujaim, Kids FM 101.7 is a revolutionary destination for Fun, Education, and Inspiration. It is conceived with a heartfelt mission at its core. "Kids FM 101.7 is designed to inspire kids and empower parents by bringing out the best of their abilities and providing easy access to education and entertainment on radio, " the CEO said.

More than just a radio station, Kids FM 101.7 is envisioned as a vibrant community where children find a voice and a space that respects their perspectives, nurturing their innate curiosity.
