Last month, Payne opened up in an interview with Men's Health Australia about what it was like being a part of the global pop culture phenomenon that was One Direction. "Its difficult when you have the level of fame that we had in the band. There have been a lot of people in trouble with mental health that arent really getting the help that they need and I think thats a bit of a problem in our industry. Its the same shit that happens to everyone, thats been happening since the 70s. You know what the traps are and if you are lucky enough, like me, to be able to get out of that scenario and back into a sense of normality, then you know its a bit different.
