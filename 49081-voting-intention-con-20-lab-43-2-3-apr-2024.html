Voting Intention: Con 20%, Lab 43% (2-3 Apr 2024)

Latest YouGov Westminster voting intention figures

The latest YouGov/Times voting intention poll has the Conservatives on 20% (-1 from 26-27 March) while Labour's vote share has reverted three points upwards to 43%.

Last week Reform UK reached their highest ever figure of 16%, a rate which they retain in this week's poll.

Elsewhere, the Lib Dems are on 8% (-2) and the Greens are also on 8% (no change).
