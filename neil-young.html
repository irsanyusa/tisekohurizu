Neil Young (Singer-songwriter) - On This Day

Profession: Singer-songwriter

Biography: Co-founded the band Buffalo Springfield along with Stephen Stills and Richie Furay, later joining Crosby, Stills & Nash as a fourth member in 1969. His work is characterized by his deeply personal lyrics, distinctive guitar work and signature alto or high tenor singing voice.

Born: November 12, 1945
Birthplace: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
Age: 78 years old

Generation: Silent Generation
Chinese Zodiac: Rooster
Star Sign: Scorpio
