What 22 Actresses From Iconic Romantic Films of the 80s and 90s Look Like Today

You probably know that in the ’80s and the ’90s, romantic movies were really popular. At that time, there were a lot of these films. And they discovered so many new names for us: Julia Roberts, Sophie Marceau, Winona Ryder... And the list of beautiful women that made their careers thanks to romantic comedies goes on and on.

Bright Side decided to find out what the iconic actresses, who turned every new film into a really important event that became really popular at the end of the previous century, look like today.

Kim Basinger, 67 years old

Helen Hunt, 57 years old

  • Mystic Pizza (1988), Pretty Woman (1990), Dying Young (1991), I Love Trouble (1994), Something to Talk About (1995), My Best Friend’s Wedding (1997), Notting Hill (1999), Runaway Bride (1999)

Molly Ringwald, 53 years old

  • Sixteen Candles (1984), The Breakfast Club (1985), Pretty in Pink (1986), The Pick-up Artist (1987)

Mary Stuart Masterson, 54 years old
