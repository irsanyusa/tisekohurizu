Cee-C and Cross try to settle their misunderstanding on 'BBNaija All Stars'

They both explained their side of things on an issue earlier on about some food items in the house. Cee-C felt hurt about not getting a particular meal even though she had expressed wanting it and felt ignored by Cross's actions during the ordeal. Cross explained being oblivious about how she felt considering how he was also trying to settle things off during his reign as Head of House.

They made their peace as they both expressed understanding things better from the other persons point of view.

This wasn't the first misunderstanding they have had on the show, even though they had commenced the season as besties. Cee-C had earlier voiced out after winning the Black Envelope immunity how painful it was to learn that Cross had put her up for eviction last week.

They had also argued about Cross boycotting her during his BBF selections, which she expressed was due to the distraction he had when Kimoprah came into the house.
