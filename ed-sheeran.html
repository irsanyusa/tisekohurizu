Ed Sheeran (Singer-Songwriter) - On This Day

Profession: Singer-Songwriter

Biography: One of the most successful artists of the 21st century, Sheeran has released three studio albums to date: "+" (plus), "x" (multiply) and "%" (divide). These albums have been incredibly successful commercially and have been critically well-received.

Sheeran began recording music in 2004, and his popularity abroad began in 2012 when he made a guest appearance on Taylor Swift's album Red and then toured with her.

Since his success Sheeran has launched his own record label, Gingerbread Man Records.

Born: February 17, 1991
Birthplace: Halifax, England, United Kingdom
Age: 33 years old

Generation: Millennial
Chinese Zodiac: Goat/Sheep
Star Sign: Aquarius
