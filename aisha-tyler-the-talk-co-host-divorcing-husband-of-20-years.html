'The Talk' co-host divorcing husband of 20 years

TMZ reports that the couple have been separated forever a year, having separated back in January 2015. There's no reason yet as to why Tietjens filed for divorce this month [April] but he cites irreconcilable differences.

The site claims he did not request spousal support, but reserved the right to do so in the future, reports. However, Tyler’s rep tells the outlet that she and Tietjens intend to stay friends as their relationship dates back more than two decades.

The college sweethearts met at Dartmouth and got married in 1994. And while they have no children, she has openly discussed her struggle to conceive on “The Talk.”
