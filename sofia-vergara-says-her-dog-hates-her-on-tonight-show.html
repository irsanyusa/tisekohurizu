Sofia Vergara appeared on The Tonight Show with Jimmy Fallon to promote the upcoming season of America’s Got Talent. While speaking to the late night host, Vergara admitted that her dog ‘hates’ her. The TV personality also opened up about her thoughts going into her second season of AGT.

Sofia Vergara Speaks on her Dog, Husband

Sofia Vergara introduced her dog Bubbles to Jimmy Fallon viewers during an interview with the host:

“Yeah, that’s Bubbles. She hates me,” Vergara admitted, shortly after Fallon held up a picture of the pet. “She was supposed to be for me, she was supposed to be my dog and she arrived at the house and I don’t know, she thought she was for Joe and that’s all she wants to do, and she’s like after him all day long.”

Vergara continued her hilarious rant, going as far as detailing the family pet ‘getting angry’ when she walks into the room.

“It’s an obsession this dog has with him,” she said.

Fallon continued the interview, asking Vergara about the difference in music taste she has compared to her husband, Joe.

“Joe is a little more.. out-there than me,” she laughed. “It’s like music that is coming out of the devil’s house.”

Fallon confirmed with Vergara that her husband is a metal rock fan, vastly different to Vergara’s preference for ‘simple Latin music’.

Sofia Vergara Reveals Favorite Act on ‘AGT’

Vergara later went on to recap the previous season of America’s Got Talent with both Heidi and Simon being absent from the show at different points: “Heidi got sick, Simon got in a horrific accident, he couldn’t walk and it was like a whole ordeal.”

America’s Got Talent will return next week with Vergara as judge for the second time after her debut appearance last year. Also returning are judges, Heidi Klum, Howie Mandel, and Simon Cowell.

“The show is great,” she said. “I mean I love the dancers. I realized how much I love watching people dance. It’s really my favorite.”

Catch America’s Got Talent when it returns June 1 on NBC.
