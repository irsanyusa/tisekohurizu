15 Pics That Show What Really Happens Behind the Scenes of Famous Movies

Actors in the movie The Irishman were de-aged and a real girl in Alita: Battle Angel was transformed into a computer-generated character. We only see the results of the work of a professional filmmaking team, but it’s also interesting to see what actually happens behind the scenes.

We at Bright Side decided to check and see what goes on backstage during the filming of famous movies, and we’d like to share our findings with you.

1. An actress in Alita: Battle Angel was generated into a computerized character.

2. The actors in The Irishman were de-aged using the digital mapping of faces with dots.

4. The effect of water in Aquaman was perfectly visualized after using a blue screen.

5. It was lots of fun behind the scenes of The Old Guard.

6. Timothée Chalamet shares the backstage happenings of Little Women.

8. Disney uncovers the backstage happenings of Maleficent: Mistress of Evil.

9. This is what the elephant actually looked like in Dumbo.

11. Here is our favorite friendly cast from Game of Thrones.
